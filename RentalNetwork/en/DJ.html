House/Progressive

DJ at Rental Network Norway

City: Oslo, Norge

Born: Oslo, Norge

Dj Tommy Schou is born and raised in Oslo. As long as he can remember the music has always been one of the biggest and most important parts of his life. At the age of 16 like most others, he got into the music genre House. A few years later he got his debut at Oslo's famous club XLNT. After that it only went one way. Further down the road he attended Club Cayenne, Gabler, Stardust, Opera bar, Pulse(Fredrikstad), Nexux, Naken and Tryvann in 2012. One of the reasons for his success and one of his main trademarks is that he has the ability to play music after what the crowd wants. He never plans a show but analyses the atmosphere and plays therefrom. He is always up to date with the new released house, and plays a good combination between old classics, new hits and some fantastic mashups. Besides all he produces his own music and has among other things made two songs for the russ.
