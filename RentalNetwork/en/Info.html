What do we do?

Rental Network Norway supplies manpower and equipment for festivals, concerts, corporations and private events. We have a top of the line and very flexible (equipment base)?, which enables us to easily fulfill any type of contract. We strive to create new and innovative solutions, as well as close relations with the customer before, under and after the event. Our vision is to create unique and safe experiences for everybody. With our 4 employees, and a broad selection of freelance/technician personnel, we ensure you get experienced and reliable technicians and project managers that are eager to to help you and make your event memorable. We are very easily accessible with our 24-hour support-line we are always ready to assist you if a problem should arise.
